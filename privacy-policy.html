> Privacy Policy — Maze Mutt

Effective date: July 14, 2026 · Published by Levanter

The short version

Maze Mutt collects no data. None. Not from you, not about you.

Data collection

Maze Mutt does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information or usage data. The app contains no analytics, no advertising, no tracking technologies, and no third-party SDKs that collect data. The app makes no network connections.

Local storage

Your high scores are saved only on your own device so the app can display them to you. This data never leaves your device, is not accessible to us or anyone else, and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Children's privacy

Because Maze Mutt collects no data from anyone, it collects no data from children.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app ever changes any of the above, this policy will be updated before that version is released, and the effective date above will change.
