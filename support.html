> Ascii Games — Support

Published by Levanter

Ascii Games is a retro terminal-styled arcade: five games drawn entirely in ASCII characters — Maze Mutt, Block Drop, Sudoku, Word Search, and Slides (15 Puzzle).

Frequently asked

Where are my high scores kept?
On your device only. Deleting the app deletes your scores.

Does the app need an internet connection?
No. Everything works fully offline.
