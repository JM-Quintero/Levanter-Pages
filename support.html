> Maze Mutt — Support

Published by Levanter

Maze Mutt is an endless survival game drawn entirely in ASCII characters: a labyrinth falls down the screen in waves, and you steer a scrappy terminal-born dog up through its corridors before the walls sweep you off the bottom.

Frequently asked

Where are my high scores kept?
On your device only. Deleting the app deletes your scores.

Does the app need an internet connection?
No. Everything works fully offline.

Do walls hurt the dog?
No — walls only push. The run ends when you're swept off the bottom of the screen. Escape sideways.

Found a bug? Have a suggestion?

Email jesusmquintero3@gmail.com and include your device model and iOS version if you're reporting a bug.
